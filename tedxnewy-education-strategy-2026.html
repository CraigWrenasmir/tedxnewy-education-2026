Education
Strategy 2026
TEDxNewy Program Year: 2026 Status: ACTIVE PLANNING Lead: Education Arc
//==[ OVERVIEW.TXT ]==================================================================================//

In 2026, TEDxNewy will expand its civic impact through a connected Education Arc that links youth engagement, university collaboration, and the main October TEDxNewy event. The goal is to cultivate young voices, strengthen institutional partnerships, and amplify ideas that shape Newcastle's future.

Rather than focusing solely on speaker training, this strategy emphasises collaborative idea generation, multiple forms of expression, and cross-generational dialogue.
